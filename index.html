The Genius Who Planned Through Time

Welcome to my HTML Interactive Story!

This is a simple game where you guide the choices of Blue, a time traveller trying to save their colleague from work.

Instructions

Nothing too complex for the instructions!

Scroll on this story box to read through the story.

Click on the buttons below to make a decision!

Sometimes, you'll find yourself going through the same areas in the story. That's fine! Repetition is a core feature of the story and how you go about it is up to you

Oh! and when you press `Start`, don't close this tab to experience and control the music!

Finally! This story breaks in Internet Explorer 8, so perhaps not use that and go for a up-to-date browser!
